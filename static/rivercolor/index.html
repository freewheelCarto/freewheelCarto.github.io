Rivers of Color

Mapping rivers and streams named for colors.

After planning a paddling trip down the Black River in South Carolina, and talking about another trip to a swimming hole on Black Creek in South Carolina, I wondered how many other rivers and creeks were named for colors, and if there were any geographic patterns. I knew of a handful of others, being a river enthusiast, and I figured there were many more that I did not know.

The map below is what I found using a US wide dataset from NOAA . I queried for the colors below, and in several languages to catch things like the Blanco River, or River Rouge. I also search for several 'stream' synonyms, such as 'creek', 'stream', 'branch', 'run', etc... and one that was new to me, 'coulee'.

I tallied the length of the different streams. Variations of 'White' and 'Red' were nearly even with greater than 7,000 km (4,350 miles). Of course I am sure I missed some steams, and the data used are not likely exhaustive. If you see something missed, have a comment, or see any geographic patterns in the data, let me know.
