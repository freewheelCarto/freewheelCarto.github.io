------ GIS ------

I spent the last decade as a GIS Manager for the South Carolina Department of Natural Resources.

I now collaborate with the great folks at Community Geographics.

I like high quality cartography, scripting with Python, and modeling GIS workflows. I've worked in just about every corner of Esri's ArcGIS platform, and also with QGIS and other open-source tools.

The conservation, environmental, natural resources, and landscape monitoring spaces in GIS are where my mind has been since college, but I like to learn new things.

If you are in need of custom cartography or expertise in GIS, you can send me an email to get in touch. I'll be glad to hear from you!

------ About ------

Tanner Arrington | Columbia, South Carolina

A lot of the things I enjoy are map-centric, like exploring on my bicycle, floating and fishing rivers, hiking, camping, and wandering around. So I also like maps, and at work I make maps (among other things).

I like to ride bikes and sometimes I write about it:
