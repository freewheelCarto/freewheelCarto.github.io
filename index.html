About

I am a professional in GIS and cartography. These are some of the things I do outside of my day job. I love how maps, both static and interactive, can communicate information uniquely and effectively. For this stuff on the side, I try to use open source products like QGIS, Inkscape, and Leaflet. I try to contribute some to this community, mostly by giving feedback on projects like QGIS plugins or leaflet features through GitHub.

A lot of the things I enjoy are map-centric in many ways, such as exploring on my bicycle, floating and fishing rivers, hiking and camping, and exploring new cities and towns.
