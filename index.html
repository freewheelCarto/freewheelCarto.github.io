------ About ------

Tanner Arrington | GIS and cartography | Static and web | Columbia, South Carolina
Here you find some examples of the work I've done, either at work or on my own.

I love how maps, both static and interactive, can communicate information uniquely and effectively. Both at work and on the side, I use the ESRI suite of software plus open source products like QGIS, Inkscape, and Leaflet.

A lot of the things I enjoy are map-centric in many ways, such as exploring on my bicycle, floating and fishing rivers, hiking and camping, and exploring new cities and towns.
